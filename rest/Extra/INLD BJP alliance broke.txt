INLD and BJP had alliance in Haryana which was broken in 2009. Both parties had alliance for Lok Sabha elections but for Vidhan Sabha elections of 2009, BJP contested alone. The alliance was particularly good for BJP as INLD had strong foothold in Haryana. In 2009 elections INLD also proved better than BJP as INLD won 31 seats with vote percentage 25.79% of whie BJP won 4 seats with vote percentage of 9.04%. The breaking of alliance also took away INLD strong voter's base fo BJP
